Saturn

The Ringed Giant

  • Saturn is a captivating gas giant, the sixth planet from the Sun in our solar system.
  • Saturn's claim to fame is its magnificent ring system, consisting of countless icy and rocky particles that encircle the planet, creating a breathtaking celestial spectacle.
  • This colossal planet is the second-largest in our solar system, with a diameter of about 116,464 kilometers (72,367 miles).
